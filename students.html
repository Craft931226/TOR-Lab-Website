For prospective students

Who we are looking for

  • Background in oceanography, atmospheric science, climate science, or related engineering fields.
  • Experience with programming (Python/Matlab), data analysis, or numerical modeling.
  • Interest in typhoon–ocean interaction, marine heatwaves, satellite remote sensing, or ocean mixing.
  • Collaborative mindset and strong communication skills.

How to apply

Email the PI with:

  • 1-page statement of interest describing your research goals and fit with typhoon/ocean topics.
  • CV/resume and transcripts (unofficial are fine for initial contact).
  • Links to publications, code repositories, or writing samples (optional).
  • Potential funding sources or fellowships you are interested in.

FAQ

Do I need prior field experience?
No, but willingness to participate in field campaigns is important.
Are remote collaborations possible?
Yes, depending on project needs; some projects require on-site work during campaigns.
Do you take summer interns?
We host a limited number of summer students; contact us early in the year.
