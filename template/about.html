About the lab

Typhoons are among the most destructive natural hazards. About 30 form annually in the western North Pacific, posing major risk to East Asia. The lab uses multi-satellite remote sensing (SST, SSH, surface winds), in-situ ocean observations, and numerical models to study typhoon?cean interaction and air?ea fluxes, and to assess how warming oceans alter energy supply, cyclone activity, and air?ea exchanges.
