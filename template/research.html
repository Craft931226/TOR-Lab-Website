Research focus

Typhoon-Ocean Coupling

Air-sea fluxes, upwelling, and mixing to explain cyclone intensification or decay and improve intensity forecasts.

Marine Heatwaves

Assessing how extreme SST events supply energy and shape typhoon intensity evolution.

Upper-Ocean Thermal Structure

Estimating subsurface heat content from altimetry and SST to support intensity and climate risk assessments.

Internal Waves & Cooling

Studying nonlinear interactions between internal waves and cyclone forcing to explain regional cooling patterns.

Shallow-Water Interaction

Analyzing nearshore shallow-water environments where depth and topography constrain intensification and cooling.
